🛡 COMPOSITE VERIFICATION

Phone+DOB · Email+Code · Name+BirthDate IDOR

📞 Phone + Date of Birth

Verify agent identity using phone number and date of birth combination.

✉ Email + Verification Code

Verify using email and verification code. Code pattern: agent_id × 1111.

👤 Name + Birth Date

Verify personnel using full name and date of birth.

ℹ Composite IDOR Notes

Composite identifiers combine two or more known fields to authenticate access. Despite appearing more secure, they remain IDOR vulnerabilities because:

  • Phone numbers are often publicly discoverable
  • Dates of birth are low-entropy (365 × ~50 years)
  • Verification codes may follow predictable patterns
  • Full names combined with DOB are insufficient for true identity verification
